Welcome to Last.fm Treemap!

Last.fm Treemap generates a treemap chart based on your scrobbles, where the size of each rectangle is based on how much you listened to that artist/album.

Choose Your Experience

Lightweight Version

+ Fetches top albums and artist immediately.

+ Easily export your chart as an image.

- Lacks interactive features.

Interactive Version

+ View all your scrobbles.

+ Click on an Artist to see its Albums, and click on an Album to see its Tracks.

+ More specific filters and options.

- Long loading times.

- Prone to API errors.
